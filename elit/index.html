About the Artist

Khalid Kura is a photographer based in Philadelphia, PA. In his early years he was an illustrator before taking an interest in photography. Originally from Brooklyn, NY, Kura has gained inspiration from both cities. His love for street photography and architecture started in NYC. Observing the different ways of life and many amazing structures captured his imagination. He utilizes the natural beauty of life and people to create amazing compositions and art that will stand the test of time. He has experience working with models, various events, children and industry professionals. Once you choose him for your photography needs you will be happy to know he is dedicated to making your vision a reality.

“Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever… It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything.” - Aaron Siskind
